Graphing Calculator

In the 2024-25 Advanced Object-Oriented Design (AOOD), we excitedly learned abstract classes through a series of assignments where we made Grapher classes. We later had the optional extension to build a Swing-based UI.

FAQ

We began exploring abstract classes by diving in head-first and writing some ourselves. The Grapher assignments were intended to teach us the basics of Java's Abstract Classes. We made classes for different types of graphers, such as XYGrapher, FunctionGrapher, PolynomialGrapher, and others!

Yes, we in fact had a log during the entirety of our two-ish weeks on this project. During the last five minutes of class, we would note down in detail the things we accomplished, needed to do, and had questions/complaints about. The log was an excellent move by our teacher to keep us moving and stay focused during class time.

Due to time constraints, the final two assignments for this project—which involved prototyping a UI digitally or on paper and then coding and integrating that UI with the existing Grapher assignments—were made optional. I chose to complete these assignments due to extra time I had in class.
