Chez Game

In the 2024-25 Advanced Object-Oriented Design (AOOD), we made games (usually modeled off of chess in some way) to help us learn interfaces. All piece abilities (movement, jumping, hopping, etc.) needed to be abstracted into interfaces.

FAQ

In stage one in our design process for this game, we paired up into groups of two (my group was special and was a group of three) and worked together on creating the rules of our game. Being a group of three, our teacher added the extra challenge of having to code our app into Java Swing, whose UI we planned in this stage.

During stage two, we implemented our game mechanics and tested it via the console. Being a group of three with the teacher's challenge, we also finished off the Java Swing aspect in this stage as well.

Our game's ingenuitive and groundbreaking name is simply a slight modification from "Chess" a very unique, well-thought-out name that we carefully handpicked and selected to be our game's name.
